# MindLearn Video Demo Script

Target length: 3 to 5 minutes.

## 0:00 - 0:25: Intro

Hi, this is MindLearn, an educational technology project for West Hacks. MindLearn helps students understand why a study session feels effective, overloaded, avoidant, or unfocused by modeling cognitive load, motivation, stress, sleep, and learning behavior over time.

## 0:25 - 1:05: Problem

Students usually track assignments and grades, but those do not explain what is happening during the learning process. A student might study for hours and still feel stuck, or avoid starting because the workload feels too big. MindLearn gives students a simple way to reflect on their learning state and get useful study strategies.

## 1:05 - 2:05: Walkthrough

On the dashboard, we can see three main scores: cognitive load, learning efficiency, and emotional friction. The graph shows how those scores change over time.

Now I will submit a check-in. I can rate stress, focus, motivation, and energy, then add study hours, sleep hours, and a journal reflection.

After submitting, MindLearn updates the dashboard, detects a learning pattern, shows the trend, and recommends specific study strategies.

## 2:05 - 3:10: How It Works

The core logic is deterministic. The journal text goes through a rule-based feature extractor that looks for signals like self-criticism, comparison, overload, and disengagement.

Then the scoring engine computes cognitive load, learning efficiency, and emotional friction. The pattern engine detects patterns like overload, avoidance loop, perfectionism, or inefficient studying. The recommendation engine maps those patterns to study strategies.

AI is only used after this process to explain the already-computed profile in simple educational language. It does not make the scores or recommendations.

## 3:10 - 4:00: Why It Matters

MindLearn helps students make better study decisions. Instead of just saying "study more," it can suggest smaller blocks, active recall, easier starting steps, or progress tracking depending on the detected pattern.

## 4:00 - 4:30: Closing

MindLearn fits the education theme because it makes learning more reflective, organized, and effective. Thank you for watching.
